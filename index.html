Daily News Bulletin

Date: January 6, 2025

Massive Earthquake Shakes the Region

A powerful earthquake struck early this morning, causing widespread panic and significant damage in several areas. The earthquake, measuring 7.8 on the Richter scale, hit at 4:30 AM, leaving residents rushing to safety.

Authorities have urged people to remain calm and follow safety protocols. Emergency response teams are working around the clock to rescue those affected and assess the damage. Many buildings have been reported damaged, and there have been disruptions in power and water supply in some regions.

The government has set up relief camps and is providing necessary assistance to the affected people. Citizens are advised to stay updated on news and avoid traveling to the affected areas unless absolutely necessary.

Safety Tips During an Earthquake


  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On until the shaking stops.

  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects that could fall.

  • If outside, move to an open area away from buildings and power lines.
